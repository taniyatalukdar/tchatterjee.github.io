About

Cake might not be an Indian invention, but it has become an integral part of any and every celebration. Be it a birthday, wedding, anniversary or a farewell, the occasion remains incomplete without a cake. We understand this very requirement and offer custom made cakes for our clients to celebrate different occasions.

Quality is our utmost concern and keeping that in mind along with the taste of our clientele, we use the best of products that are most of the times sourced from out of India. We don’t limit ourselves when it comes to creativity as we feel that variety is the spice of life. Having said that, we don’t believe in mediocrity and don’t let our work to be defined by the price.

If you are looking for custom homemade cakes that are prepared with care you have reached the right place! And, not just cakes, we love making cupcakes, cake-pops, and chocolates too. Next time around if you are looking to gift a bouquet to your loved one, be different and try our specialty chocolate bouquets, we guarantee you that you’ll be left asking for more…
